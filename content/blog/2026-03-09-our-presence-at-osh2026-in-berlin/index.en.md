This year in Mai (23-24.05.2026), theOpen Hardware Summit (OHS 2026), is organised in Berlin, in the main building of the TU Berlin.
The Open.Make project (and our partner project [LAUDS Factories](www.lauds.eu)) will be presented quite extensively, and a specific 3 ECTS course module at the TU Berlin will bring students in contact with open source hardware topics.

This year at the
Pentecost weekend in Mai (23-24.05.2026), the TU Berlin main building
will serve as an international hub for the open hardware community.
The Open Hardware Summit (OHS 2026), organized by the Open Source
Hardware Association (OSHWA), will take place in Germany for the
first time in 2026.
The Open.Make project (and our partner project
LAUDS Factories) will be presented quite extensively.

We are indeed
involved in the organisation as the local partner, proposing a
specific course for TU Berlin students (this allowed to grow our
teaching port-folio and provide the TU Berlin rooms for free).
15
students will be selected as part of an open call.


Around 300
participants from all over the world are expected to attend,
including developers, artists, start-ups, researchers, and makers who
are enthusiastic about open technologies.
As in the past, the two-day
program will feature lectures, hands-on workshops, exhibitions and
arts installations featuring open hardware and diversity in the maker
scene, thereby actively pushing the topics of feminism and
decolonisation.
Topics such as sustainable hardware development,
repairability, open-source chips, and community-driven manufacturing
and production will be the focus.
In addition, there will be
networking formats and local satellite events in Berlin's maker and
hacker spaces as well as at our institute.

For instance, we
will run a satellite workshop on Friday 22nd to develop business
model framework for LAUDS Factories.
While we are still discussing
whether the [BUA Mobile Lab](../../../../../mobilelab) will be exhibited during the event, we
will be present with one talk and one workshop.

We are looking to see you there !
